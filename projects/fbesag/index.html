fbesag is a non-stationary extension of the classical Besag model, developed to model spatial variation across heterogeneous sub-regions. It supports structured random effects with varying precision and is fully compatible with the INLA framework.

📄 Paper

The method is described in the article:
Non-stationary Bayesian spatial model for disease mapping based on sub-regions
Published in Statistical Methods in Medical Research, 2024.

🔍 Applications

fbesag has been successfully applied to disease mapping in Brazil and is ideal for:

  • Public health risk modeling
  • Non-stationary spatial smoothing
  • Bayesian inference over irregular areal domains
